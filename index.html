About Us
Built on accessibility and excellence

The Article 19 mission

Article 19 was founded in 2023 and has grown into one of the most respected MUN-based organizations in the Lahore circuit. Our mission is to make high-quality MUN training accessible to students across cities free of cost, while also offering exclusive delegation discounts. We bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity by creating an environment where delegates can learn, compete, and grow without financial barriers standing in the way.

Our delegates have represented Article 19 on renowned platforms including LUMUN, FMUN, GTI MUN, and Ittehad MUN, consistently bringing home credible awards and recognition for analytical rigor and eloquence.

Diversity & Inclusion

Bringing together voices from different backgrounds to promote collaborative and innovative problem-solving.


Diversity and inclusion are not merely ideals but essential pillars for sustainable global progress. In a world interconnected by trade, technology, and shared challenges, embracing cultural, ethnic, and ideological differences strengthens international cooperation. True inclusion ensures that every voice regardless of gender, background, or belief is heard and valued in decisionmaking processes. For the United Nations and its member states, fostering such an environment is not only a moral imperative but also a strategic necessity to achieve peace, equity, and innovation.

Conference Policy
No coat, no coach policy

Article 19 MUN commits to registered attendees only, strict delegate and staff conduct, controlled movement, and careful use of halls, classrooms, furniture, and equipment.

We also coordinate closely with venue administration, maintain smooth operations, and ensure responsible handover after each day.

Dedicated organizing and operations teams supervise access, respect venue timings and policies, and keep the conference institution-friendly at every stage.

The goal is simple: a secure, orderly, and high-quality MUN experience for every delegate.
